# Conclusions

The analysis provides valuable insights into customer behavior and business performance.

Key findings include:

* A measurable percentage of customers have churned, indicating opportunities to improve retention.
* Customer churn varies across demographic groups such as income level, education, and marital status.
* Blue card holders represent the largest customer segment and contribute significantly to overall transaction activity.
* Customers with higher transaction amounts generate the majority of revenue.
* Certain customer groups exhibit higher inactivity, making them more likely to churn.
* Customers with higher credit limits generally contribute greater transaction revenue.
* Customer engagement metrics such as transaction count and relationship count help identify loyal customers.

These findings can help the bank prioritize retention campaigns, improve customer engagement, and maximize revenue from existing customers.
