# Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The following KPIs are used throughout the dashboard:

## Customer KPIs

* Total Customers
* Existing Customers
* Churned Customers
* Churn Rate (%)

## Revenue KPIs

* Total Transaction Revenue
* Average Transaction Amount
* Average Transaction Count

## Credit KPIs

* Average Credit Limit
* Average Credit Utilization
* Average Open to Buy Amount

## Customer Engagement KPIs

* Average Relationship Count
* Average Months on Book
* Average Inactive Months
* Average Contacts with Bank

## Segmentation KPIs

* Customers by Gender
* Customers by Age Group
* Customers by Income Category
* Customers by Education Level
* Customers by Card Category
* Customers by Marital Status

These KPIs provide a comprehensive overview of customer behavior, profitability, and churn patterns.
