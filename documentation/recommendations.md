# Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis, the following recommendations are suggested:

## 1. Reduce Customer Churn

Develop targeted retention campaigns for customer segments with above-average churn rates.

## 2. Reward High-Value Customers

Introduce loyalty programs and exclusive offers for customers with high transaction amounts and frequent card usage.

## 3. Increase Customer Engagement

Encourage inactive customers to use their credit cards through cashback offers, reward points, and promotional campaigns.

## 4. Upgrade Eligible Customers

Identify customers with high spending and strong repayment behavior and offer upgrades to premium credit cards.

## 5. Improve Customer Communication

Monitor customer contact frequency and proactively engage customers showing signs of reduced activity.

## 6. Personalized Marketing

Use demographic insights such as income category, age group, and card category to design personalized marketing campaigns.

## 7. Monitor Customer Health

Regularly track KPIs such as churn rate, transaction revenue, credit utilization, and customer engagement to support data-driven decision making.

Implementing these recommendations can improve customer retention, increase transaction revenue, and strengthen long-term customer relationships.
