# Business Problem

Banks invest significant resources in acquiring new credit card customers. However, customer attrition (churn) reduces profitability and increases customer acquisition costs. Understanding why customers leave and identifying the characteristics of high-value customers are essential for improving customer retention and maximizing revenue.

This project analyzes a bank's credit card customer dataset to answer key business questions using SQL in BigQuery and interactive dashboards in Looker Studio.

The analysis focuses on:

* Measuring the overall customer churn rate.
* Identifying customer segments with the highest churn.
* Understanding customer demographics and spending behavior.
* Analyzing transaction revenue across different customer groups.
* Providing actionable recommendations to improve customer retention and increase business revenue.
